Eta Theta Tau

Eta Theta Tau - The official app for HθT, the songfest group at Pepperdine University

The app can be downloaded on the App store here.

Songfest is a yearly mini-musical competition held at Pepperdine University. Eta Theta Tau is one of the 7 current performing groups. Check out http://www.pepperdinesongfest.com/ for more info.

App Features

• Get info about daily rehearsals • Stay connected with HθT Leadership • Get notifications with updates about the show • Access lyrics to the song from the show • Read the script and practice lines • Check out videos of the choreography • See the "Hottie of the Day"
